Data & Processing Architecture

From device telemetry to analytics.

  • Data sources: MongoDB and SQL Server.
  • Scheduled pipelines for daily ingestion and scoring.
  • Historical storage for trend analysis.
  • Analytics layer powered by Power BI.

Key Technical Highlights

Engineering choices that shaped the solution.

Weighted health scoring

70% hardware signals and 30% connectivity indicators for balanced prioritization.

Daily historization

Time-series storage to compare current status against long-term behavior.

Data quality metrics

Freshness, completeness, and anomaly flags embedded in the pipeline.

Critical asset ranking

Automatic prioritization for field teams and maintenance planning.

Maintenance-first design

Focused on reducing downtime and guiding preventive actions.
